Welcome to Jiangmei

Jiangmei Village is in the southwest of Qianqian Village at the southernmost tip of Baisha Township, Penghu Island and is surrounded by the ocean. It was originally called "Gangwei" in Hokkien dialect because the bay to the north of the village was the end of the town's seaport. Since the villagers' ancestors built small houses near the coast and gradually became a settlement; therefore, it was named "Gangwei" Village. In 1955, after the resolution of village assembly, it was renamed “Jiangmei” meaning “passing on legends, and advocating the virtues” in Mandarin Chinese to sound more meaningful and beautiful.
